    Laboratories:

    The laboratories of any school are the heart of the study of science subjects. The study of science subjects without laboratory is vague, uninteresting and incomplete. All laboratories are equipped with necessary equipment for all experiments of class 11 and class 12 as per the present syllabus of Uttar Pradesh Board of Secondary Education. The school has a separate laboratory for high school classes where students learn the theoretical parts of the curriculum through experiments.
